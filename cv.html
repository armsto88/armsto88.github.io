Summary

Professional profile

I'm an ecologist with a strong technical background who works at the intersection of biodiversity monitoring, conservation technology, eco-acoustics, and environmental data tools. My work spans field ecology through to hardware prototyping, data pipeline design, and interactive ecological dashboards.

I'm based in Germany and work internationally, with experience across European conservation and monitoring contexts. I'm particularly interested in building systems that close the loop between field observation and ecological insight — practical, reliable, and usable by the ecological teams who need them.
